Finding A Wedding Planner

Advice From The Knot

Start With Your Style

Before you look for a wedding planner, think about your wedding size, style and where you’d most like an expert’s opinion.
Do you want help with everything or just day-of details? The best wedding planner for you will understand your style and needs.

Understand the Options

The type of wedding planner you hire will depend on the skills and level of support you need.
A coordinator will step in closer to your wedding date, while a wedding planner will help you plan it all from start-to finish.
